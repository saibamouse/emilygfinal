My Project Idea

Girl2Girl - A social-media and connection service made for women!

Overview
Girl2Girl is a social media platform app for making friends. It allows women to connect to other women exclusively in an environment that makes them feel safe and secure. These connections are made through tags (on the user’s profile description) which contain a variety of hobbies that can help them to find other women that share the same mutual interests too. This app will cover more features, such as the standard video-calls, texts, gifs and emojis which will be one of the main focuses in terms of communication. Another communication method will be introduced in the form of a forum which allows for large public discussions on certain topics.



Motivation
The motivation for this app is driven by the fact that a large majority of women face sexual and/or violent harassment both online and in person. Studies show that in the US, 65% of women have experienced street harassment. However, the statistics grow in other countries such as Thailand and Brazil where the percentage of street harassment is up to 86%. In Delhi, India, infamously known as “the rape capital” about 63% have been subjected to unwanted staring, 50% were inappropriate comments, 40% have been touched/groped/grabbed and lastly 26% have been followed. (Borker 2017, p.8) Consequently, with the rise of social media becoming the new norm for social interaction this harassment has already translated to the online space making it difficult for women to feel safe anywhere. My proposition is to make an online safe-space for women to come together freely without the fear of harassment. Hopefully this app can help to reduce the amount of online harrassment and harrassment in person globally.
